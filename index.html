Get to know me

Born and raised in Hong Kong, studied and lived in New Zealand since 2003 until 2013.


After graduation I moved back to Hong Kong, spent 16 months working on the West Kowloon LawCourt Building project. The real world experience in a professional setting opened up new perspectives and made me realize I'm capable and can acheive much more. So I decided to explore new career options dedicated on solving the biggest issues.

In 2017, I came to the conclusion that the advancement in technology like Artificial Intelligence and Automation will have a massive impact and reshape the world in the near future. As a result I fully committed into programming and begin learning to code for the first time in my life. "There is a whole section on this below"

In mid 2019, China ended Hong Kong's democracy, western values along with the freedom we once had under British rule. I took part in all the peaceful protests and attempted to defend our home by all means. Things went from bad to worse with the Covid pandemic later that year, fearing for the endless lockdown in China. My top priority was to find a way out of the city while ensuring the safety of my family.

In late 2021, after a busy year of preparation, I moved to the UK permanently with my family. Resuming a normal life post Covid.
